Create a dummy.bat file in C:\ with contents "pause"
Download NSSM from http://www.nssm.cc/download
Extract nssm.exe (x86 or x64) to C:\Windows\nssm.exe
Run "nssm install"
Path: C:\dummy.bat
Service name - As you wish!
Hit "Install service"
After that, you'll have a service named after step 6, which you can start, stop, restart...
